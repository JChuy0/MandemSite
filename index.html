About Local Mandem:

At Local Mandem, we get it. Reaching out for mental health support isn't always easy. That's why we created a simple, discreet way to connect you and your community with the help you need, right when you need it. Our QR code stickers are more than just cool designs; they're lifelines. A quick scan instantly links to a curated list of vital mental health resources. Stick 'em anywhere, share 'em with your mandem, and let's make mental wellness accessible to everyone.
